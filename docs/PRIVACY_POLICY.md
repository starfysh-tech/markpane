# MarkPane Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

## Overview

MarkPane ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MarkPane handles your information.

## Information Collection

**MarkPane does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### What We Don't Collect:
- No user accounts or login information
- No personal data
- No analytics or usage statistics
- No crash reports (unless you manually submit via Apple)
- No cookies or tracking technologies
- No advertising identifiers

## Data Processing

MarkPane operates entirely on your local device:

- All markdown files are processed locally on your Mac
- No files are uploaded to external servers
- No network connections are made except when:
  - Loading external images referenced in your markdown files
  - Rendering mermaid diagrams (processed locally)

## File Access

MarkPane requests permission to:
- Read markdown files you explicitly open
- Access files via Quick Look preview extension

All file access is:
- User-initiated
- Limited to files you select
- Processed locally on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers

## Third-Party Services

MarkPane does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

MarkPane does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no age-restricted content.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date.
